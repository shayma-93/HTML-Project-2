Seoulful Food

Appetizers

  • Bibim Mandu (Korean Potsticker Salad)

    Dumplings,lettuce ,cabbage,carrot mixed with Korean chilli paste (gochujang),rice vinegar and soy sauce drizzled with sesame oil and sprinkled with sesame seeds.

  • Gimmari (Fried Seaweed Roll)

    Dried seaweed, glass noodles,and optional vegetable ingredients (e.g. garlic chives or carrots).

  • Sotteok (Rice Cake and Sausage Skewers)

    Korean rice cake and cocktail sausage mixed with ketchup, soy sauce and gochujang (Korean chili paste).

Main Courses

  • Bibimbap

    A wholesome rice bowl of classic bibimbap vegetables, beef and a gochujang sauce!
    Note: You can omit the meat and egg for vegetarian or vegan bibimbap.

  • Bibim Guksu (Spicy Cold Noodles)

    Bibim guksu is a cold noodle, made with thin wheat flour noodles or buckwheat noodles, dish with fresh vegetables served in a spicy, sweet and tangy gochujang sauce.

  • Kimchi Bokkeum Bap (Kimchi Fried Rice)

    Rice, well fermented kimchi and meat (bacon, ham, sausage, or tuna) fried with soy sauce and korean chilli paste

Desserts

  • Bukkumi (Rice Cake Dumplings with Sweet Red Beans)

    Lightly pan-fried stuffed with sweet red bean paste rice cake dumplings

  • Dasik (Tea Cookies)

    These traditional tea cookies are made mainly from either sesame seeds or rice flour or soyean flour.

  • Yakgwa (Honey Pastry)

    This sweet pastry is made into layers, deep-fried and soaked in a rich ginger and honey syrup.
